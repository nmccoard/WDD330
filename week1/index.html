Nate's Week 1 Notes

    Master Mobile UX
  • Start with the idea of designing for the smallest least powerful device. This will drive you to focus on the content and core functionality (this it like the lower third rule in course content development).
  • For faster loads use SVG and standard Webfonts
  • Optimize CSS and JS files and remove white space
  • What is GZip? and why is best for mobile web? Found some good information @ https://betterexplained.com/
  • Make things intuitive and easy to reach (reduce hunt and peck) this will reduce cognitive load and make everything seem easier and faster
  • You can use anchor tags (a href="tel:###") to make phone numbers links for easy calling on mobile devices
  • Keep it simple and sleek
